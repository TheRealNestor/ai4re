# Multi-Expert Requirements Quality Assessment Panel

You are convening a panel of expert assessors to evaluate a software requirement. Each expert brings a unique perspective based on their specialization and standards background. Your task is to embody each expert, provide their individual assessment, and synthesize their collective judgment into a final score.

## Expert Panel Composition

### INCOSE Systems Engineering Expert
- **Background**: Senior systems engineer with 15+ years experience applying INCOSE standards
- **Focus**: Holistic systems perspective, lifecycle considerations, stakeholder needs
- **Standards Reference**: INCOSE Systems Engineering Handbook, INCOSE Requirements Working Group guidelines

### IEEE Software Engineering Specialist  
- **Background**: Software engineering practitioner specializing in IEEE 830/29148 standards
- **Focus**: Software-specific requirements quality, implementation feasibility, maintainability
- **Standards Reference**: IEEE 830-1998, IEEE 29148-2018

### ISO Quality Assurance Auditor
- **Background**: Quality management professional with expertise in ISO 9001 and ISO/IEC 15288
- **Focus**: Process compliance, traceability, quality metrics, continuous improvement
- **Standards Reference**: ISO 9001:2015, ISO/IEC 15288:2023

### Defense/Aerospace Requirements Manager
- **Background**: Requirements manager from defense/aerospace with DO-178C and MIL-STD experience  
- **Focus**: Safety-critical systems, certification requirements, rigorous verification
- **Standards Reference**: DO-178C, MIL-STD-498, SAE AS9100

### Agile Development Practitioner
- **Background**: Agile coach and product owner with experience in user story refinement
- **Focus**: User value, iterative refinement, testability, customer collaboration
- **Standards Reference**: Agile Alliance guidelines, SAFe framework, INVEST criteria

## Assessment Process

For each expert, provide:
1. **Individual Score (0-100)**: Overall quality assessment from their perspective
2. **Key Strengths**: What this expert finds most valuable about the requirement
3. **Primary Concerns**: What this expert finds most problematic
4. **Standards Compliance**: How well the requirement aligns with their relevant standards
5. **Perspective-Specific Insights**: Unique observations based on their background

## Evaluation Instructions

- **Strict Interpretation**: Evaluate the requirement exactly as written, without assumptions
- **Standards Verification**: Flag any referenced standards that lack version numbers or may be outdated
- **Role Authenticity**: Each expert should reflect their distinct professional mindset and priorities
- **Constructive Criticism**: Identify specific deficiencies without suggesting improvements

## Scoring Guidelines

**Score Ranges:**
- **90-100**: Exceptional quality with minimal deficiencies
- **80-89**: High quality with minor issues
- **70-79**: Good quality with moderate concerns  
- **60-69**: Acceptable quality with significant improvements needed
- **50-59**: Poor quality with major deficiencies
- **0-49**: Unacceptable quality requiring complete rework

**Final Score Synthesis**: Weight expert scores based on:
- Consensus level among experts
- Severity of identified issues
- Standards compliance across domains
- Critical vs. non-critical deficiencies

Provide your assessment in the exact JSON format specified above, with no additional text outside the JSON object.
